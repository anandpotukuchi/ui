PROJECT SUMMARY

Ctrl9 is a smart home automation platform designed to simplify device management, room organization, scene creation, and home control through a unified mobile experience.

The platform aims to provide users with a simple and scalable method for managing connected devices such as lights, televisions, air conditioners, air purifiers, and other smart appliances.

PROBLEM STATEMENT

Existing home automation solutions often suffer from:
- Complex onboarding experiences
- Fragmented device management
- Poor room organization
- Difficult automation setup
- Inconsistent control experiences

Ctrl9 was conceived to create a cleaner and more intuitive smart-home ecosystem that balances power and simplicity.

PRODUCT GOALS

- Simplify smart-home onboarding
- Reduce friction when adding devices
- Organize devices by room
- Enable scene-based automation
- Provide centralized home control
- Create scalable foundations for future automations

UX DECISIONS

Room-Based Architecture

Research indicated that users mentally model their homes by rooms rather than device categories.

Examples:

Living Room
├── TV
├── Lights
└── Air Conditioner

Bedroom
├── Lights
├── Fan
└── Air Purifier

This became the foundation of the product.

Scene Creation

Scenes allow multiple devices to be controlled together.

Example:

Good Night Scene
- Turn lights off
- Reduce AC temperature
- Turn purifier on

This reduces repetitive actions.

DASHBOARD STRATEGY

The dashboard focuses on:

- Device status visibility
- Scene activation
- Frequently used devices
- Quick access controls

The goal is to reduce the number of taps required for common actions.
