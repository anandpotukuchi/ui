PROJECT OVERVIEW

Date: May 2024
Status: Work in Progress (Abandoned)
Role: Product Designer, UX Designer, Design Engineer
Project Type: Conceptual Redesign

PROJECT SUMMARY

This project reimagines the Indian Railways and IRCTC digital ecosystem as a unified travel platform rather than a standalone train-booking application.

The redesign introduces a modular ecosystem where multiple travel-related services operate under a common design language:

- Rail Travel
- My Bookings
- Wallet & Payments
- Tarang (Hospitality)
- Tiffino (Food & Catering)

The objective was to simplify the traveler experience and unify disconnected services into a single journey-centric platform.

PROBLEM STATEMENT

Current railway digital experiences often separate services into independent workflows:

- Ticket booking
- Food ordering
- Hotels and retiring rooms
- Lounges
- Insurance
- Wallets

Users must navigate multiple interfaces and mental models to complete a single trip.

The redesign seeks to create a travel ecosystem centered around the user's journey.

PORTFOLIO ASSESSMENT

Overall Rating: 8.8 / 10

The redesign successfully reframes Indian Railways as a digital travel ecosystem rather than a ticket-booking application. The strongest aspects are product strategy, service integration, journey-centric thinking, and information architecture. Future iterations would benefit from expanded onboarding, accessibility validation, and further refinement of brand identity.
